Rachel Doherty | About Me

My background and practical objectives for this course

Who am I?

Originally from Nottingham, UK and now living in Vienna, Austria I currently work for a private university within the Managing Directors office. I previously held roles in startups and in the pharmaceutical industry as an admin professional/ project coordinator. I am creative and have many interests including music & arts, sports and learning languages. I speak German and Italian.

Why Computer Science?

I am studying Computer Science to better understand the field as we move the university more towards online learning and EdTech. In addition, as a university specialising in innovation & entrepreneurship, I also want to gain a solid technical understanding of programming, app and software development and data science. As learning programming languages/ techniques and to efficiently process data becomes more prolific in professional life for many individuals, I see a great opportunity to upskill here in order to take the next step in my career.

What do I want to achieve?

I do not necessarily intend to become a Computer Scientist, Software or App Developer but I would like to develop the following competencies:

  • Develop analytical skills

  • Understand the rationalisation and anlysis of data

  • Learn a programming language

  • Understand the design and development of computer software to achieve strategic organisational goals.
